The University of Sharjah is an Emirati private national
university located in University City,
Sharjah, United Arab Emirates. It was established in October
1997 by its founder the ruler of Sharjah Sheikh Dr.
Sultan bin Muhammad Al-Qasimi to meet the emirate of
Sharjah's aim of educational needs.

Abu Dhabi University is a private university in the United Arab
Emirates with campuses in Abu Dhabi, Al Ain and Dubai.
It was established in 2003, after three years of planning by Hamdan Bin Zayed Al Nahyan
and other citizens of the United Arab Emirates. According to the University, its founders
"envisioned an institution that would be among the best in the UAE and throughout the world.
The largest private university in the United Arab Emirates, it offers undergraduate and postgraduate
degrees based upon the American model of higher education. Its founder and current Chairman is
Ali Saeed Bin Harmal Al Dhaheri.

Khalifa University is a public research university

by QS world university rankings of 2023.
